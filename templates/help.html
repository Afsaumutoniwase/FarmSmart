Help & Support

Here you can find useful resources and support for your account.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I change my password?

To change your password, go to the settings page and enter your current password, then provide your new password and confirm it. Your password will be updated once submitted.

How can I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to the settings page and click "Delete Account". Be aware that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Where can I find learning resources?

Access our comprehensive learning resources by clicking on 'Resources' in the sidebar. You'll find guides, tutorials, and educational content to help you master hydroponic farming techniques and best practices.

How do I participate in the forum?

To participate in the forum, go to the 'Forums' section from the sidebar. You can browse categories, create posts to ask questions, share experiences, and reply to other farmers' discussions.

How do I use the AI Nutrient Analysis?

Go to your dashboard and select your crop type (Lettuce, Peppers, or Tomatoes). Enter your sensor readings including pH level, EC value, and temperature. Click 'Analyze with AI' to get instant, intelligent recommendations for your hydroponic system.

How can I access the EzaSmart chatbot?

The EzaSmart Assistant chatbot is available on every page. Look for the green chat icon with our logo in the bottom corner of your screen. Click it to ask questions about hydroponics, get farming tips, or troubleshoot issues 24/7.

Contact Support

If you can't find the answer to your question or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to us!
