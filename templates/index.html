Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about FarmSmart's technology and services.

What is FarmSmart and how does it benefit smallholder farmers?

FarmSmart is a platform designed to help smallholder farmers increase productivity through advanced technologies like hydroponics and smart irrigation. We provide tools that help farmers use resources efficiently, improve yields, and adapt to challenges like water scarcity and unpredictable weather.

How does FarmSmart's hydroponic system work?

Our hydroponic system allows crops to grow without soil by using nutrient-rich water. This method optimizes space usage and reduces water consumption by up to 90% compared to traditional farming, making it ideal for regions with limited arable land or water.

Is FarmSmart available offline?

Yes, FarmSmart offers offline functionality to ensure farmers in rural areas with limited internet access can still use the platform. Once data is synced online, updates and analytics are seamlessly integrated.

What kinds of crops can be grown using the hydroponic system?

The hydroponic system supports a wide range of crops, including leafy greens (like lettuce and spinach), herbs, tomatoes, strawberries, and peppers. Our system is adaptable to different climates and growing conditions.

How can FarmSmart help me save water while farming?

FarmSmart's smart irrigation system uses sensors to monitor soil moisture and weather conditions. The system ensures water is only used when necessary, which reduces water usage by up to 50%, making it highly efficient for water-scarce regions.

What kind of support does FarmSmart offer?

We offer ongoing support through local agricultural extension workers and provide access to expert consultants. Our team is available 24/7 to help with setup, troubleshooting, and training on how to best use our tools and technologies.
